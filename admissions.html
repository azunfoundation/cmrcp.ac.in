Admission Procedure

Admissions into UG and PG programs at CMR College of Pharmacy are made as per the guidelines of the Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE).

1

Entrance Examination

Candidates must appear for TS EAMCET (for B.Pharm & Pharm.D) or TS PGECET/GPAT (for M.Pharm) and secure a valid rank.

2

Document Verification

Attend the certificate verification process at designated help line centers as notified by the Convener.

3

Web Counseling & Seat Allotment

Exercise web options by selecting CMR College of Pharmacy (College Code: CMRP). Seat allotment will be done based on rank, category, and preferences.

4

Reporting to College

Download the allotment order, pay the fee online, and report to the college with all original certificates for final enrollment.
