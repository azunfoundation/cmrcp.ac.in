B.Pharmacy HOD

Home / B.Pharmacy HOD

Dr. G. S. Sharma

M. Pharm., Ph. D Professor, Pharmaceutics

Professor  &  UG & NBA Coordinator

Dr. G. S. Sharma, Professor at CMR College of Pharmacy, has obtained his B. Pharm (2003) He qualified at GATE-2004 and completed his M. Pharm (2006) from Andhra University, Visakhapatnam and obtained Ph. D degree in Pharmaceutical Sciences from JNTU Hyderabad in 2013. He has 19 years of teaching and administrative experience at various levels of B. Pharmacy, Pharm D. and M. Pharmacy. He has published 54 research papers in National and International journals, 10 book chapters and 2 patents to his credit. He also guided 19 M. Pharm students for their project work in JNTU Hyderabad.

Dr. Aleti Raja Reddy

M. Pharm., Ph. D. Professor

HOD Department of Pharmaceutical Chemistry

Dr. ALETI RAJA REDDY is working as Professor in department of Pharmaceutical cmeistry. He has 17 years of teaching and research experience. He has about 54 research paper publications in reputed journals, 10 book chapters and 1 patent to his credit. He has presented research papers in about 16 National & International conferences across India. He has guided about 18 B. Pharm Projects and 21 M. Pharm Projects. He has attended 13 workshops and 12 faculty development programs in India. He is a life member of APTI & IACP.

Dr. V. Venkata Rajesham

M. Pharm., Ph. D. Professor

HOD Department of Pharmacology

Dr. V. Venkata Rajesham is currently working as Professor in the Department of Pharmacology. He has 18 years of teaching and research experience. He has Published 60 Research articles in reputed National and International journals, 9 book chapters and 4 Patents to his credit and guided 21 M. Pharmacy projects. He is the training and placement officer from CMRCP. He is coordinating the placements in various pharmaceutical domains. He is also the life member of Indian Pharmaceutical Graduates Association.

Dr. Jilla Lavanya

M. Pharm., Ph. D Associate Professor

HOD Department of Pharmacognosy

Dr. Jilla Lavanya is currently working as Associate Professor in the Department of Pharmacognosy. She has 12 years of teaching and research experience. She was awarded with Ph. D degree from Osmania University, Hyderabad in 2021. She worked as a Project Assistant-III for 4 years in IICT-Hyderabad under DST-SERB (GAP-0424). She published 20 research papers in reputed international journals and presented a poster presentation in International conference held at IICT-Hyderabad. She has 6 book chapters and 1 patent to her credit.

Dr. N. Madhavi

M.Pharm., Ph. D. Associate Professor

HOD Department of Pharmaceutics

Dr. Nimmathota Madhavi is currently working as an Associate Professor in the department of Pharmaceutics with 9 years of teaching and 5 years of full-time research experience. She was selected for WOS-B in the year 2013, from Ministry of Science and Technology, New Delhi and the recipient of Research Fellowship from UGC, New Delhi during Ph. D and received a gold medal for best Ph. D thesis in the department of pharmaceutical technology at 85th & 86th convocation held on 09-01-2019. She published 57 papers in various reputed National/ International journals indexed with Scopus, Springer, Taylor and Francis journals. She published 1 book and 10 book chapters and 2 patents. She has guided 12 M. Pharmacy projects till date.

Dr. P. Kishore Kumar

M. Pharm., Ph. D Professor

HOD Dept. of Pharmacy Practice

Dr. P. Kishore Kumar is working as an Professor in the department of Pharmacy Practice with 12 years of teaching and research experience. He completed Ph. D from Jayprakash Vishwavidyalaya IN 2017, Patna. He published 42 research articles in various reputed International & National journals. He also attended 17 International and National level conferences. He guided 10 PG Projects, he has 6 book chapters and 1 patent to his credit.

CMR College of Pharmacy is dedicated to fostering professional graduates with consistent quality education, training, and research to serve industry, environment, and society.
