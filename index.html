Who We Are

Welcome to
CMR College of Pharmacy

Established in 2005 under the MGR Educational Society, CMRCP has grown into one of the most preferred institutions for pharmacy education in Telangana. Our state-of-the-art campus provides the perfect environment for academic excellence and research innovation.

  • Research & Innovation

    Advanced laboratories and SIRO recognition promoting cutting-edge pharmaceutical research.

  • Industry Connect

    Strong ties with top pharmaceutical companies for training and placements.

  • World-Class Campus

    Smart classrooms, CPCSEA approved animal house, and in-campus hostels.
