Our History

A Legacy of Excellence

CMR College of Pharmacy (CMRCP) was established in the year 2005 under the aegis of MGR Educational Society. Located in the serene environment of Kandlakoya, Hyderabad, the college is a constituent of the prestigious CMR Group of Institutions.

Approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and Pharmacy Council of India (PCI), New Delhi, the college is permanently affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad (JNTUH). Our B.Pharmacy program has been accredited by NBA since 2018.

The institution has been recognized under UGC 2(f) and 12(B) and is recognized as a Scientific and Industrial Research Organization (SIRO) by DSIR, Government of India. CMRCP consistently features in the NIRF Rankings, placing in the 101-125 Rank Band in NIRF 2025.

Vision

"To be the most preferred institution for education in pharmacy in this state."

Mission

  • Foster professional graduates with consistent quality education, training and research to serve industry, environment and society.
  • Inculcate leadership, teamwork and professional ethics.
  • Make students globally competitive.

Welcome to CMR College of Pharmacy. In today's rapidly evolving healthcare landscape, the role of a pharmacist has expanded from dispensing medications to becoming an integral part of the healthcare team. At CMRCP, our endeavor is to provide an educational environment that fosters innovation, professional skills, and ethical values.

With our state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and strong industry linkages, we ensure our students receive comprehensive training. Our NBA accreditation and consistent NIRF rankings reflect our commitment to quality education.

I invite you to join us and embark on a rewarding journey in the noble profession of pharmacy.
