Facilities

World-Class Campus

Our campus is equipped with modern infrastructure designed to foster learning, creativity, and holistic student development in the field of pharmacy.

Smart Classrooms

Spacious, well-ventilated classrooms equipped with LCD projectors, smart boards, and continuous student monitoring systems for interactive learning.

Wi-Fi Enabled ICT Facilities

Laboratories

State-of-the-art labs for Pharmaceutics, Pharmaceutical Analysis, Pharmacology, Pharmacognosy, and an advanced Central Instrumentation Room.

HPLC/GC Machine Room

Animal House

A dedicated animal house facility approved by CPCSEA (Committee for the Purpose of Control and Supervision of Experiments on Animals) for pharmacological research.

Hostel Facilities

In-campus secure residential accommodation for both boys and girls with modern amenities, study rooms, and hygienic mess facilities.

24/7 Security Wi-Fi

Transport

An extensive fleet of college buses providing safe and convenient transport facility covering all major routes across Hyderabad and Secunderabad.

Extensive Coverage

Canteen

A spacious and hygienic cafeteria serving a variety of nutritious meals and snacks at subsidized rates for students and staff.

Sports Complex

Excellent indoor and outdoor sports facilities including cricket, basketball, volleyball, and a well-equipped gymnasium to promote physical fitness.

Medicinal Garden

A well-maintained medicinal plant garden with rare and diverse species of medicinal plants for Pharmacognosy practicals and research.
