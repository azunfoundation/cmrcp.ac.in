Bridging Academia and Industry

The Training and Placement Cell at CMR College of Pharmacy is dedicated to ensuring our students are industry-ready. We facilitate campus interviews, organize workshops, and provide comprehensive soft skills and technical training.

Our strong industry-institute interaction ensures that our curriculum aligns with current market needs, resulting in excellent placement records year after year with top pharmaceutical and healthcare companies.
