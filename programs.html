Bachelor of Pharmacy

Duration 4 Years (8 Semesters)
Intake 100 Seats
Accreditation NBA Accredited

The B.Pharmacy curriculum is designed to produce a scientifically and technically competent pharmacist who can apply this education in such a manner as to provide maximum health care services to patients.

Apply Now

Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

  • To provide comprehensive pharmacy education with strong foundational concepts.
  • To prepare students for successful careers in pharmaceutical industry, research, and clinical settings.
  • To develop professional ethics, communication skills, and lifelong learning attitudes.

Eligibility

  • Intermediate (10+2) with Bi.P.C. / M.P.C.
  • A valid rank in TS EAMCET.
  • 70% admissions under Category-A (Convener Quota).
  • 30% admissions under Category-B (Management Quota).

With over 20 years of academic and research experience, the HOD leads a team of highly qualified faculty dedicated to shaping the future of pharmacy students. The department focuses on outcome-based education and practical skills.

Master of Pharmacy

Duration 2 Years (4 Semesters)
Level Postgraduate

M.Pharmacy is an advanced program that enables students to specialize in a specific field of pharmacy. The course includes rigorous coursework followed by a year-long research project (dissertation) in industry or academia.

Apply Now

Specializations Offered

Pharmaceutics

Focuses on formulation development, novel drug delivery systems, and industrial pharmacy.

Intake: 15 Seats

Pharmaceutical Analysis

Training in advanced analytical techniques, method development, and quality control.

Intake: 15 Seats

Pharmacology

Study of drug action, pre-clinical screening, toxicology, and molecular mechanisms.

Intake: 15 Seats

Eligibility

  • B.Pharmacy degree from an AICTE/PCI approved institution with at least 50% marks.
  • A valid rank in TS PGECET or GPAT.

Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm.D)

Duration 6 Years
Intake 30 Seats

Pharm.D is a 6-year professional doctorate degree program consisting of 5 years of clinical and academic study and one year of internship/residency. It emphasizes clinical pharmacy, patient care, and ward rounds.

Apply Now

Program Highlights

  • Direct patient care and ward round participation.
  • Training in hospital pharmacy, clinical toxicology, and pharmacotherapeutics.
  • One-year mandatory internship in a multi-specialty hospital.

Eligibility

  • Intermediate (10+2) with Bi.P.C. / M.P.C.
  • A valid rank in TS EAMCET.
  • 70% admissions under Category-A (Convener Quota).
  • 30% admissions under Category-B (Management Quota).

CMR College of Pharmacy is dedicated to fostering professional graduates with consistent quality education, training, and research to serve industry, environment, and society.
