NSS Activities

The National Service Scheme (NSS) unit of CMRCP actively engages students in social service and community development programs.

  • Blood Donation Camps

    Organized annually in collaboration with Red Cross Society, where students and staff actively donate blood.

  • Health Awareness Campaigns

    Pharmacy students conduct awareness drives on rational use of medicines, hygiene, and disease prevention in adopted villages.

  • Haritha Haram (Plantation Drive)

    Mass tree plantation programs within and outside the campus to promote environmental sustainability.
